Chocolate Chip Pancakes

Chocolate chip pancakes are a delicious breakfast or brunch treat featuring the classic flavor combination of fluffy pancakes infused with melted chocolate chips. They offer a sweet and indulgent experience with a perfect balance of soft, fluffy texture and gooey chocolate pockets.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted and cooled
  • ½ cup miniature chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Sift flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt into a separate bowl.
  2. Whisk milk and egg together in a bowl until thoroughly combined.
  3. Stir melted butter into the flour mixture, then whisk in milk mixture until smooth.
  4. Fold in chocolate chips.
  5. Heat a small nonstick skillet over low heat. Pour about 1/3 cup batter into the hot skillet and cover with a lid. Cook pancake until browned on the bottom, about 3 minutes.
  6. Flip pancake, then cover and cook until browned on the other side, 2 to 3 minutes. Remove to a plate and repeat to make remaining pancakes.
